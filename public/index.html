Our Mission

Nyumbani is Kenya's pioneer provider of HIV/AIDS care. We provide a comprehensive range of services including residential care, community outreach, and world-class laboratory testing. Nyumbani has served over 40,000 HIV-infected and affected persons and continues to support over 1,000 affected orphans and vulnerable children annually.

Our Pillars of Support

Nyumbani Children’s Home

Our flagship residential facility provides a loving family environment for up to 100 children living with HIV/AIDS. We focus on holistic care, including nutrition, medicine, and emotional support.

Nyumbani Village

Located in Kitui, this sustainable community supports HIV orphans and the elderly. It features eco-friendly housing, schools, and a farm to ensure long-term sustainability.

Lea Toto Program

A community-based outreach program providing medical and social care to over 3,000 HIV+ children in Nairobi’s informal settlements.
